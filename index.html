Maestro

Mobile based music instructor

Abstract

Maestro is a mobile based application tailored to young students, between the ages of 7 – 12, that are learning new instruments. This app will motivate them to keep practicing and allow the students to learn at their own pace without the pressure of having a set schedule.

How it Works

There are two important components as to how the app works. The first part is the interactive score sheet that the student can following along while playing their instrument, which during different modes the individual can change the pace.The second aspect of the application is its ability to listen to what the individual is playing. Then at the end the app will show a score of how well the individual did.

Reason

The idea came from a conversation the group had about having a set schedule and not being able to move at our own pace; like piano lessons. When it comes to learning a new instrument it is unanimous that children hate following a schedule and the idea of not practicing and getting penalized by your piano teacher was a nightmare. Maestro allows the student to work at the own pace but having fun at the same time. The apps ability to listen, will help students narrow down on which part of the song they need to practice on before going to their next lesson.
